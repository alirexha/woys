vcclient-cachy mic helper

Quick reminder: the local engine publishes a microphone called vcclient-mic. To use it in this tab:

  1. Make sure the engine is running (vcclient-cachy run).
  2. In the site's voice/call settings, pick vcclient-mic as your input.
  3. If you don't see it, reload the tab — browsers cache the device list.
